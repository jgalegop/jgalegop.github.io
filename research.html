My research interests include light–matter interactions in organic materials. In particular, the focus of my PhD thesis, titled "Polaritonic chemistry", is the manipulation of chemical processes and properties of organic molecules strongly coupled to confined electromagnetic modes.

If you are interested, continue reading below for a brief introduction to the field of polaritonic chemistry. If you want more information, or crave for more technical details, please feel free to download my PhD thesis (89 MB).
